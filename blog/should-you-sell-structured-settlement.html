Should You Sell Your Structured Settlement?

Updated May 2025 · 6 min read

Structured settlements can be a helpful financial resource — but life happens. You may find yourself needing fast access to cash. That’s when many people consider a lump sum payout.

When Is It Smart to Sell?

Selling your structured settlement may make sense if you're facing urgent needs such as:

  • Paying off high-interest debt
  • Funding education or a home
  • Starting a business
  • Medical or family emergencies

Is It Legal?

Yes — selling a structured settlement is 100% legal and requires court approval. This ensures your decision is in your best interest and protected by law.
