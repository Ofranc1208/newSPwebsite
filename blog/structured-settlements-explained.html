Structured Settlements Explained

Published May 2025 by SmarterPayouts

Structured settlements are long-term payment arrangements usually awarded after a personal injury or civil lawsuit. Instead of receiving one lump sum, the recipient gets periodic payments—monthly, quarterly, or annually—over a set number of years.

Why Do People Cash Out?

Life changes. Emergencies arise. Many people choose to sell all or part of their structured settlement to get a lump sum—whether it’s to pay off debt, buy a home, cover medical costs, or fund education.

Is It Safe and Legal?
